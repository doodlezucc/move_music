Third-party Personal Data Management

YouTube Music

If You log in with a YouTube Account, the Application wants to get permission to see, edit, and permanently delete Your YouTube videos, ratings, comments and captions. Only Your subscriptions, created playlists and liked videos are received but never modified.

My Service needs this data to work as one can't move or copy a thing they don't have access to. By signing into YouTube, You provide data which passes as a source to be moved or rather copied to another location, namely Your Spotify Account.

Spotify

If You log in with a Spotify Account, the Application wants to get write/delete access to Your "Your Music" library, write access to your playlists and write/delete access to the list of artists and other users that You follow. None of these are ever used to read or delete any of Your Spotify library contents.

This also is, in all aspects, a crucial part of the Service. Without a provided Spotify Account, there is no destination to which Your liked videos or rather songs as well as other playlists and followed musicians can be copied to.
